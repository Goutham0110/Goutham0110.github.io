Robocon 2022

The ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest is an Asian-Oceanian college robot competition, where robots compete to complete a task within a set period of time. The contest aims to create friendship among young people with similar interests help advance engineering.

The event took place in Thyagraj Stadium, New Delhi. As per the theme, we developed 2 robots 'Hitter' and 'Seeker'. Being a part of electronics division in Team NOVA has let me explore various fields of robotics and also gave me a notion to my own project to develop an automatic ball-tracking bot with aim-and-shoot capability.

Click here to know more about the event

Smart India Hackathon 2022

Smart India Hackathon is a nationwide initiative to provide students with a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving.

SIH 2022 has been the most fascinating event I have worked for. An engineer cannot ask anything more than to have an oppurtunity to solve real-time problems in organizations. We developed a Plagiarism checking tool to detect the novelty of articles submitted for National Digital Library of India. In this project, I got the oppurtunity to explore backend web development and web scraping technologies.

Click here to know more about the event

WeldRight | IIT Techfest 22

Weldright is one of the events in Techfest organized by IIT Bombay. Welding is a critical activity for manufacturing. The threshold for welding error is the bare minimum.

We were given an unbalanced dataset of welding activity and asked to build an ML model to predict welding defects in the materials by developing algorithms using process parameters such as ambient temperature, welding travel speed, and others. Working through this problem was extremely fun as we had to go through multiple approachs to tackle the unbalance in the dataset.

Click here to know more about the event

Electric 2-Wheeler Designing Challenge

Electric Two Wheeler Design Competition, organized by Society of Automotive Engineers of India (SAE India), tasks the students to think, analyse, design, develop, build, test and present in series of event.

A simple automation of a monotonous task can be tremondously helpful in the long run and creates a plethora of new innovations. We engineered an electric bike with a focus on business pitching and innovation, including adjustable carry-on space, intelligent ventilation system, parking and collision assistance.
